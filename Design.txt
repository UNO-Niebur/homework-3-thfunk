This game has 4 stages:

stage 0:
    Introduction
    Player starts with 15 Health

stage 1:
    Attack a guard who has 10 health. You take turn swinging randint(1,5) at eachother until a victor arises.

stage 2:
    Get to town and receive 2 full heal potions

stage 3:
    Fight a boss who has 15 health and swings at you for randint(1,8)
    You may use a heal or attack at each attack phase
